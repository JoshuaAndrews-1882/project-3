You defeat Glint and gain extra power and knowledge!

Glint tells you about the Flameseeker Prophecies which decribe the events that
had unfolded in the world of Tyria so far.
She also informs you about the treachery of the White Mantle and
their true identity...

They are a powerful race of spell casters known as the Mursaat!

She sends you to Southern Shiverpeaks to face the White Mantle and
rescue the people there.
